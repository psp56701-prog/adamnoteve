returns & refunds

It didn't work out. We get it. Here's how it works.

TL;DR

If your order arrived damaged or wrong, we'll fix it free. If you just changed your mind, you have 30 days to return unworn items in original condition for a refund (return shipping is on you). Personalized items aren't returnable.

Effective date: June 9, 2026

1. Damaged, defective, or wrong items

If your order arrived damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, we'll make it right at no cost to you. Email p.sp56701@gmail.com within 14 days of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • A photo of the issue
  • A short description of what went wrong

We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice. You won't be asked to ship the original item back unless it's for safety or quality investigation reasons.

2. Change-of-mind returns

Just didn't vibe? You have 30 days from the delivery date to request a return. Items must be:

  • Unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • In their original packaging where applicable
  • Not on the non-returnable list (see below)

To start a return, email p.sp56701@gmail.com with your order number. We'll send return instructions and a return address.

Return shipping is the customer's responsibility for change-of-mind returns. We strongly recommend using a tracked service — we can't refund items that don't reach us.

3. Items that cannot be returned

The following are made-to-order or hygiene-sensitive and are not eligible for return unless damaged or defective:

  • Personalized or custom-printed items
  • Candles and consumable goods that have been opened or used
  • Earrings, intimate accessories, and similar hygiene-sensitive items
  • Final sale items, marked as such on the product page
  • Gift cards (if and when offered)

4. Refunds

Once we receive your returned item and confirm it meets the conditions above, we'll issue a refund to your original payment method within 5–10 business days. The amount refunded will equal the price of the returned item. Original shipping charges are non-refundable except for damaged/defective/wrong items.

Depending on your bank, the refund may take an additional few days to appear on your statement.

5. Exchanges

The fastest way to "exchange" is to request a refund for the item you have, then place a new order for the size/color/product you want. This avoids you waiting for us to receive the original.

6. Lost or stolen packages

Once a package is marked delivered by the carrier, the responsibility shifts to you. If you believe a package is lost or stolen, please first:

  • Check with neighbors and household members
  • Contact the carrier with the tracking number
  • File a claim with the carrier if appropriate

If you've done all this and still can't locate the package, contact us — we'll work with you on a case-by-case basis but cannot always issue a replacement free of charge.

7. Cancellations

Need to cancel? We process orders quickly (often within minutes) so we can't always catch them before they go to production or shipping. Email p.sp56701@gmail.com as soon as possible — if we can stop it, we will.

8. Contact

Returns, refunds, or "what is happening with my order" questions? Email p.sp56701@gmail.com. We respond within 1–2 business days.

Note from your friendly developer: Adjust the windows, return-shipping policy, and exclusions to match your actual ops. Stripe and most card networks expect a clear, accessible refund policy — having one materially reduces chargeback risk.
