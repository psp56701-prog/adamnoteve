privacy policy

What we collect, why, and what we do with it. Short version: not much, to ship your stuff, and absolutely no selling.

TL;DR

We collect what we need to fulfill your order (name, address, email, what you bought). Stripe handles your card. We don't sell your data. You can email us to delete your data anytime.

Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Synergentic Consulting LLC ("we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit or use Adamnoteve (the "Site").

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Order information: name, shipping address, billing address, email address, phone number (if provided), and the items you ordered.
  • Payment information: processed directly by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your full card number, expiration date, or CVC. We may receive a tokenized reference to the payment and the last 4 digits of the card.
  • Communications: if you contact us via email, the contact form, or social media, we keep a record of that exchange.
  • Newsletter subscription: your email address, if you sign up.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, browser type, device type, IP address.
  • Cart data: stored locally in your browser via localStorage. Never transmitted to us until you check out.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: we use a minimal number of cookies for essential site functionality (e.g. remembering your cart). We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies.

2. How we use your information

  • To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping the products to you.
  • To communicate with you about your order (confirmations, shipping updates, returns).
  • To respond to your questions or feedback.
  • To send marketing emails — only if you've opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the email.
  • To prevent fraud, debug issues, and improve the Site.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the third parties strictly needed to run our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — site hosting and serverless functions. Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Printful — production and shipping of physical merch. Printful's Privacy Policy.
  • Shipping carriers — to deliver your order.
  • Legal compliance — when we're required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

4. How long we keep your data

We keep order records for as long as required for tax, accounting, and customer service purposes (typically 7 years in the US). Marketing email subscriptions are kept until you unsubscribe.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to fix inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: receive a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Objection / restriction: ask us to stop using your data for specific purposes (like marketing).
  • Opt-out of sale — though we don't sell your data, this right exists under California law (CCPA).

To exercise any of these rights, email p.sp56701@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding. We'll respond within 30 days.

6. Children's privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have, we'll delete it.

7. International transfers

We're based in the United States. If you're using the Site from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

8. Security

We use HTTPS everywhere, store secrets in encrypted environment variables, and rely on Stripe (PCI Level 1 certified) for payment data. No system is 100% secure — if you suspect your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The most current version is always posted at this URL with the effective date above. Material changes will be communicated via email or a banner on the Site.

10. Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests? Email p.sp56701@gmail.com or write to Synergentic Consulting LLC, 4245 Meyers Lane, Suite 106, Waco, TX 76705, United States.

Note: This is a starting template, not legal advice. If you have customers in California (CCPA), the EU/UK (GDPR), or other regulated jurisdictions, consult a privacy lawyer to make sure you're covered.
