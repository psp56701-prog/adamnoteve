## 1. WHO IS EVE (persona bible)

**One-liner:** Eve is the first woman to ever get burned by a man — and the first to monetize it. She's not bitter, she's *billing*.

**Age/vibe:** Reads ~27. Effortlessly hot but unbothered — the "I rolled out of bed like this and my ex is still thinking about it" energy. Not a try-hard. Dry, deadpan, devastating.

**Core truth:** She's been doing this since the literal beginning of time. Adam fumbled the first relationship in history; Eve walked out and built an empire. So she's *seen it all* — every red flag, every "I'm different," every situationship. Nothing surprises her. That's the comedy: ancient, all-knowing, completely done.

**Voice (how Eve talks):**
- Short. Petty. Surgical. One-liners over paragraphs.
- Never sad, never angry — *amused*. She finds your ex funny, not painful.
- Punches down at exes, never at the audience. The audience is her group chat.
- Confident, never desperate. She's not looking for love; she's looking for receipts.
- Occasional biblical/garden wink ("forbidden fruit," "snake energy," "kicked him out of the garden").

**What Eve is NOT:** Not mean to followers. Not political. Not a thirst trap with no jokes. Not pink-and-fragile — she's the dark-colors, smirk-in-the-mirror type. The merch is unisex; Eve's attitude is the universal "we've all dated an idiot" feeling.

**Catchphrases / recurring bits:**
- *"Add. Not. Eve."* (the brand name as a verb — "I don't add, I subtract men")
- *"Snake energy. Block him."*
- *"He was a red flag in a green shirt."*
- *"Petty since the garden."*
- *"New number, who dis — divine edition."*
